India has always held a tradition of not just worshipping but utilizing natural sources. From festivals dedicated to the sun and wind to simple methods like sun-dried food. And it is not surprising that India is rapidly adopting solar energy. With the abundance of natural resources, solar is fast becoming the popular as well as cost-effective choice for commercial enterprises.

Why is it a great choice you ask?



1. Control in your hands

Get the power (pun intended) in your hands installing solar right within your premises. You can control not just how much energy you buy from the grid, and how to use that, but also your expenses and your profits; and that is just the beginning. Get ready to rake in the benefits!


2. Being future ready

Installing solar means that you are locking in energy costs not just for now, but also for the future. Most good quality solar projects have a life of 25 years, so you are securing a part of your energy requirement for next 25 years. It is no secret that the price of electricity has been rising year-after-year. For instance, electricity prices in Bihar have recently increased by 55%1. Therefore, best hedge against continuously rising energy prices and energy risk is being Energy Independent.


3. Convenient and easy

Once installed, a solar power system requires very little maintenance. The system will provide electricity quietly and cleanly for 25 to 40 years. Many solar panels carry a 25-year warranty.
